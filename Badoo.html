Badoo is an international online community. The portal was developed by Russian entrepreneur Andrey Andreev and has existed since 2006. The company is based in London's Soho district. In April 2011, Badoo reached 114 million registered users. In addition to the web version of the community, Badoo also offers its own smartphone applications for the iPhone, Windows Phone and Android devices. The iPhone app is the most commonly used flirt app for the iPhone, while the Android users prefer two other apps before the Badoo app. Just over a year later, it was rated sixth among the world's most valuable social media brands, with a brand value of $ 3.7 billion, according to a study by the HWZ Zurich School of Economics and the BV4 Brand Value Rating Agency brand consultancy. The site is financed by a freemium model, that is, basic functions of contacting are free, optimized search functions are charged. Badoo is blocked in the United Arab Emirates and in Iran. How does Badoo work ?

  • You'll then see suggestions with people in your area. You can also start targeted searches. If you are interested in getting to know someone, you can simply contact them. The e-mail address will not be disclosed.
  • In addition, there are functions such as "direct hit". Here you get contact suggestions and can rate them.
  • Badoo can also be downloaded as an app for Android or iOS.
